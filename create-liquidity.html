Create Liquidity for Your Memecoin

Add liquidity to your token on Raydium to enable trading. Connect your wallet and follow the steps below.

How to Create Liquidity

  1. Connect your Solana wallet (e.g., Phantom) on Raydium.
  2. Select your token and pair it with SOL or another asset.
  3. Specify the amount of tokens and SOL to add to the pool.
  4. Confirm the transaction and pay the small Raydium fee.
